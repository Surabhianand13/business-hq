Before Day 1: What We Need From You

The 7-day clock starts only when we have everything we need. Delays almost always come from the client side — missing logos, undecided brand colours, no content. We solve this upfront with a structured onboarding brief sent immediately after booking:

  • Your logo files (SVG or high-res PNG preferred)
  • Brand colours (hex codes, or reference examples)
  • 3–5 websites you like the look of
  • All page copy (or confirmation that you want us to write it)
  • Any product/service photos or confirmation that stock is fine

If you're missing some of these, that's fine — we offer copywriting and brand direction as part of the Scale and Dominate packages. The key is knowing upfront so we can plan accordingly.

The Day-by-Day Breakdown

Day 1

Discovery & Strategy

We run a 60–90 minute discovery session (video call or async brief). We map your goals, audience, competitors, and conversion objectives. By end of day, we have a confirmed sitemap, content strategy, and design direction locked in.

Day 2

Design Concepts

Our designer builds the first mockup — hero section, colour palette, typography, and primary layout. AI tools (Figma AI, reference generation) accelerate ideation. You receive the first visual concept by end of day for feedback.

Day 3

Design Refinement

We incorporate your feedback from Day 2 and expand the design to remaining pages. Every section gets refined — spacing, hierarchy, micro-interactions. By end of Day 3, the full design is approved and dev-ready.

Day 4

Development Begins

Clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no bloated frameworks. We code the primary sections: hero, services, about, and key CTAs. AI code assistance (GitHub Copilot, Claude) handles repetitive logic; humans handle craft and creativity.

Day 5

Full Build + Animations

All remaining sections built and animated. Mobile responsiveness tuned across breakpoints. Contact form, CMS (if included), and any integrations (Calendly, WhatsApp, analytics) wired up and tested.

Day 6

Your Review & Revisions

You receive a private staging link. We walk you through the site on a screen-share call. Revisions are noted and implemented same-day. This is your chance to tweak, adjust, and get it exactly right.

Day 7

Launch Day

DNS configured, SSL certificate installed, final performance audit run (Core Web Vitals, PageSpeed), sitemap submitted to Google Search Console. Your site goes live. We hand over all files, login credentials, and a walkthrough recording.

The Tools That Make It Possible

Speed without quality isn't worth anything. Here are the key tools in our stack that let us deliver fast and well:

  • Figma + Figma AI — design and prototyping at speed
  • Claude & GitHub Copilot — code generation and debugging assistance
  • Linear — project tracking with day-by-day task visibility
  • Loom — async video feedback to replace slow email chains
  • Vercel / Netlify — instant deploys, zero-downtime launches
  • PageSpeed Insights + Lighthouse — performance validation before every launch

What Can Delay the 7-Day Timeline

In our experience, delays are almost always caused by one of these:

  • Missing content or assets from the client (the #1 cause)
  • Scope changes mid-project ("can we also add an e-commerce section?")
  • Slow feedback response (we give a 24-hour window for replies)

None of these are hard problems. We manage them proactively by setting clear expectations in the brief, keeping scope tight, and maintaining daily communication throughout the build. If a delay is ever on our end, we compensate with a discount — it's that simple.
