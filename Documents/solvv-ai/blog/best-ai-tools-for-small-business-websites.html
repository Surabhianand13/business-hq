AI Chatbots & Conversational Tools

01Tidio AIChatbot

Tidio combines live chat with an AI chatbot trained on your website content. It answers FAQs, qualifies leads, and books appointments — all automatically. The setup is simple enough for non-technical users and the free plan handles up to 50 AI conversations per month, making it perfect for small businesses just getting started.

Pricing: Free plan available · Paid from $29/mo
02Intercom FinEnterprise Chat

Fin is Intercom's AI agent built on GPT-4. It handles complex support queries with high accuracy, escalates to a human when needed, and integrates with your CRM and help docs. Overkill for a basic site, but if you're handling hundreds of support queries per month, Fin pays for itself in hours saved.

Pricing: Starts at $39/mo · Fin AI from $0.99/resolution

AI SEO Tools

03Surfer SEOContent SEO

Surfer analyses the top-ranking pages for your target keyword and tells you exactly what to write — word count, headings, NLP terms, internal links. Its AI outline generator creates a full article structure in seconds. If you're creating blog content (and you should be), Surfer is the closest thing to a guaranteed ranking formula.

Pricing: From $89/mo (worth every cent for consistent content)
04SE Ranking AITechnical SEO

A more affordable alternative to Ahrefs with solid AI features. It audits your site for technical SEO issues, tracks keyword rankings globally, and now includes an AI writer for generating meta descriptions and title tags in bulk. Great for businesses managing multiple pages.

Pricing: Free trial · Paid from $52/mo

AI Analytics & Personalisation

05Microsoft ClarityFree Analytics

Clarity is completely free and provides heatmaps, session recordings, and an AI Copilot that summarises user behaviour in plain English: "28% of users rage-clicked the contact button on mobile." This kind of insight used to cost thousands. Now it's free and takes 5 minutes to set up.

Pricing: 100% free forever
06Hotjar AIUX Research

Hotjar's AI Surveys feature automatically generates user research questions based on your page content and summarises the responses into actionable insights. Combine it with heatmaps and you have a complete picture of why your website is or isn't converting.

Pricing: Free plan · Paid from $32/mo

AI Content & Copywriting

07Claude (Anthropic)Copywriting

Claude is our go-to for website copywriting. It understands nuance, writes in brand voice, and produces long-form content that doesn't read like it was written by a robot. Feed it your brief, brand guidelines, and competitor examples — you'll get copy that genuinely sounds human. We use it for hero headlines, service descriptions, and blog outlines.

Pricing: Free plan available · Pro at $20/mo
08JasperMarketing Copy

Jasper is purpose-built for marketing teams. It has templates for every marketing format — landing pages, email subject lines, social ads, product descriptions. The brand voice training is excellent, meaning after a few sessions it writes consistently in your tone. Best for teams producing high volumes of marketing content.

Pricing: From $49/mo · Team plans available

AI Image & Design Tools

09Midjourney v7AI Imagery

For custom hero images, section backgrounds, and brand visuals that look nothing like stock photos, Midjourney v7 is extraordinary. We use it for client projects to generate bespoke imagery that matches brand colours and mood. The quality in 2026 is virtually indistinguishable from professional photography for many use cases.

Pricing: From $10/mo · Basic plan covers most SMB use cases
10Canva AI (Magic Studio)Design Automation

Canva's Magic Studio lets you generate social media graphics, resize designs for any platform automatically, remove backgrounds, and write ad copy — all inside the same tool you probably already use. For small business owners who need to create marketing assets without a designer, this is the single most valuable AI tool you can add today.

Pricing: Free plan · Pro at $15/mo

How to Choose the Right Tools for Your Business

Don't try to implement all 10 at once. Here's a prioritised approach based on business stage:

  • New business (0–6 months): Microsoft Clarity (free), Tidio free plan, Canva AI — zero cost, immediate impact
  • Growing business (6–24 months): Add SE Ranking, Claude Pro, Midjourney — invest in content and design quality
  • Scaling business (24+ months): Surfer SEO, Hotjar, Intercom Fin — optimise conversion at scale
