Speed: There's No Contest

A traditional agency typically takes 8 to 16 weeks from initial brief to launch. That timeline includes rounds of stakeholder meetings, design concept presentations, revision cycles, development sprints, and QA testing — all managed by a team where bandwidth is constantly split across multiple client projects.

An AI-powered agency like Solvv AI compresses this to 7 days. Here's why that's possible and legitimate:

  • AI tools accelerate the design mockup phase from days to hours — Midjourney, Figma AI, and Claude can generate and iterate on visual concepts at machine speed
  • Component libraries and AI code generation tools like GitHub Copilot and Claude eliminate repetitive coding tasks
  • AI copywriting assistance speeds up content creation without sacrificing quality
  • Streamlined client communication (async, structured briefs) removes meeting overhead
  • Smaller, specialist teams move faster than large agency structures

The 7-day timeline isn't cutting corners — it's cutting waste. Every step that doesn't add value to the final product has been engineered out of the process.

Cost: Dramatically Lower, But Know Why

Traditional agencies charge $10,000 to $80,000+ for a marketing website. That price reflects their overheads: office space, large teams, account managers, project managers, lengthy sales processes, and margin.

AI-augmented agencies operate leaner. At Solvv AI, a fully custom, animated, SEO-optimised website starts at $999. That's not a template slapped together — it's a hand-coded, unique site built faster thanks to AI-assisted workflows.

Quality: AI Augments, Humans Decide

The biggest misconception about AI-powered agencies is that the output is generic. In reality, AI tools are used to accelerate execution, not to replace creative direction. A skilled human designer still makes every visual decision — AI just removes the mechanical parts.

What this means for you: you get the speed of automation with the quality of human craft. Custom animations, bespoke layouts, original copy — all of it still human-directed and quality-checked.

💡

Pro tip: Ask any agency for a portfolio of recent work. The final output quality is what matters — the tools used to get there are secondary.

Customisation: Fully Custom vs Template-Based

Some people assume that fast delivery means template-based design. At Solvv AI, every site is built from scratch in clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no WordPress themes, no Wix drag-and-drop, no page builder bloat. The speed comes from the workflow, not from reusing generic designs.

Traditional agencies may also use templates (many do, at any price point). The key question to ask any agency: "Is this a custom build or a theme customisation?"

The Features Traditional Agencies Can't Match

This is where AI agencies genuinely pull ahead. Because AI is central to how we work, it's also central to what we build. Standard features at Solvv AI include:

  • AI chatbots trained on your business FAQs and product catalogue
  • AI-powered lead qualification — your site qualifies leads before they hit your inbox
  • Automated follow-up sequences triggered by website behaviour
  • AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) so your site gets cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity
  • Performance optimisation built in from day one (Core Web Vitals, lazy loading, CDN)

A traditional agency would quote these as separate, expensive add-ons. For us, they're standard.

When Should You Choose a Traditional Agency?

To be fair: traditional agencies still make sense in specific scenarios.

  • Enterprise-scale platforms: If you need a custom CMS, complex integrations with legacy systems, or a team of 10+ managing the project long-term, a large agency with deep bench strength is appropriate
  • Regulatory-heavy industries: Compliance-intensive sectors (healthcare, finance) may require extensive legal review of every design element — a process that takes time regardless of tooling
  • Long-running brand campaigns: If you need ongoing creative production (TV ads, billboard design, full brand systems), a traditional agency with a broad creative team is better suited

For the vast majority of businesses — startups, SMEs, service providers, e-commerce brands, SaaS products — an AI-powered agency delivers a superior result at a fraction of the cost and time.

The Verdict

The web design landscape has fundamentally shifted. AI tools haven't made designers obsolete — they've made great designers 10x more productive. The agencies that have embraced this shift can now deliver better work, faster, for less money.

"The best tool for the job isn't the most expensive one. It's the one that ships the fastest and performs the best."

If you're evaluating agencies right now, the question isn't "AI or traditional?" — it's "who will deliver the best result for my budget and timeline?" In 2026, for almost every business, that answer is an AI-powered agency.
